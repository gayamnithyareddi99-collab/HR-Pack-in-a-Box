About HR Pack-in-a-Box

HR Pack-in-a-Box is a modern HR infrastructure platform designed to help startups and growing organisations simplify human resource operations. The platform provides structured systems for employee documentation, HR workflows, compliance management and operational HR processes.

By combining digital tools, structured workflows and secure documentation systems, HR Pack-in-a-Box helps organisations build reliable HR foundations without complex systems or large internal HR teams.

The platform is built to support fast-growing teams that need efficient HR operations while maintaining compliance, organisation and transparency across their workforce.

Founder Vision

HR Pack-in-a-Box was founded by Rohith Ragi with the goal of making HR infrastructure simple, accessible and structured for startups and emerging companies.

The vision behind the company is to provide organisations with practical HR systems that help manage employee operations, documentation and compliance through a clear and organised digital framework.
